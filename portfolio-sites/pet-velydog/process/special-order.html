Process/Special Order

Special Order

Looking for a specific breed, size, or color that is not currently listed on our website? Velydog offers a special order service through our trusted network of ethical breeders in Korea. Tell us what you are looking for, and we will work to find your perfect match.

How It Works

Special Order Process

01

Share Your Preferences

Contact us with details about the puppy you are looking for. This includes breed, size preference, color, gender, and any other specific requirements. The more detail you provide, the better we can match you with the perfect puppy.

02

We Search Our Network

Our team will reach out to our trusted network of ethical breeders across Korea to find puppies that match your criteria. We only work with breeders who meet our strict health, welfare, and ethical standards.

03

Meet Your Match

Once we identify potential matches, we will share photos, videos, and health information with you. You can schedule video calls to meet the puppies virtually before making your decision.

04

Reservation & Delivery

After you select your puppy, the standard reservation and delivery process applies. A 50% deposit secures your puppy, and we handle all logistics for safe international delivery to your home.

Timeline

What to Expect

Special orders typically take longer than selecting from our current listings. Here is a general timeline:

  • 1.Initial consultation: 1-3 days
  • 2.Puppy search & matching: 1-4 weeks
  • 3.Video call introduction: Within 1 week of match
  • 4.Reservation & preparation: 1-2 weeks
  • 5.Delivery (after meeting CDC requirements): 2-8 weeks
Pricing

Custom Requirements

Pricing for special orders varies based on:

  • Breed rarity and demand
  • Size specifications (teacup, toy, miniature)
  • Color and coat preferences
  • Gender preference
  • Breeder availability

We will provide a detailed quote after understanding your specific requirements. There is no obligation until you confirm the reservation.
