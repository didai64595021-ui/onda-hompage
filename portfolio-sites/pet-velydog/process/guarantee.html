Process/Health Guarantee

1-Year Health Guarantee

At Velydog, we stand behind the health of every puppy we place. Our comprehensive 1-year health guarantee provides you with peace of mind, knowing that your new family member is protected against genetic and congenital health conditions. Your puppy's well-being is our highest priority.

Every Puppy Includes

Health Package

Up-to-date vaccinations per international veterinary guidelines
Complete deworming treatments
Microchip identification (ISO 15-digit standard)
Comprehensive veterinary health examination
Full vaccination and medical records
Dietary recommendations and feeding guide
1-year health guarantee certificate
Lifetime support from the Velydog team
Coverage Details

What's Covered

Genetic Disorders

Coverage for hereditary conditions that may manifest within the first year, including hip dysplasia, patellar luxation, and heart defects.

Congenital Defects

Protection against birth defects that were not detectable at the time of adoption, including organ malformations and skeletal issues.

Infectious Diseases

Coverage for major infectious diseases contracted within the first 14 days of arrival, indicating pre-existing exposure before delivery.

Parasitic Conditions

Treatment coverage for internal parasites detected within the first 30 days, as part of our comprehensive deworming protocol.

Claims Process

What to Do If Your Puppy Gets Sick

01

Contact Us Immediately

If your puppy shows any signs of illness, reach out to us via WhatsApp or email as soon as possible. Early communication is key to resolving health concerns quickly.

02

Visit a Licensed Veterinarian

Take your puppy to a licensed veterinarian for a professional examination. Keep all receipts, medical records, and diagnostic reports for your claim.

03

Submit Documentation

Send us the veterinary report, diagnosis, and any test results. Our team will review the case and determine coverage under the health guarantee.

04

Resolution & Support

Based on the veterinary findings, we will work with you to provide the appropriate support, which may include reimbursement of medical costs, a replacement puppy, or ongoing treatment guidance.

Our Partners

Veterinary Partnership

Velydog works closely with licensed veterinary professionals in Korea to ensure the highest standards of health care for our puppies. Every puppy in our care receives regular health monitoring, preventive treatments, and thorough examinations before joining their new family.

We also maintain relationships with veterinary clinics in major destination cities and can provide referrals to trusted veterinarians near you for ongoing care after your puppy arrives home.
