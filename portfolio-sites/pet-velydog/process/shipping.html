Step by Step

The Shipping Process

01

Pre-Flight Health Check

Every puppy undergoes a comprehensive veterinary examination within 72 hours of departure. This includes a full physical assessment, final vaccination verification, and issuance of an internationally recognized health certificate. Our partner veterinarians ensure each puppy meets all requirements for safe international travel.

02

Government Quarantine Inspection

Before leaving Korea, all puppies pass through an official government quarantine inspection. This mandatory step ensures compliance with both Korean export regulations and the destination country's import requirements. All documentation is prepared and verified by our experienced logistics team.

03

Temperature-Controlled Transport

Puppies travel in specially designed, IATA-approved pet carriers with climate control. The cargo hold is maintained at a comfortable temperature throughout the flight. Each puppy has adequate space, ventilation, fresh water, and comfortable bedding for the journey.

04

Real-Time Tracking & Updates

From the moment your puppy leaves our facility until arrival at your local airport, you will receive real-time updates via WhatsApp. We provide flight details, tracking numbers, estimated arrival times, and photo updates at each stage of the journey.

05

Airport Pickup & Handover

Upon arrival, your puppy will be available for pickup at the designated cargo area of your local airport. We provide detailed pickup instructions, required documentation, and support throughout the customs clearance process. Your puppy arrives ready to go home with you.

Important Note

Delivery Timeline

Due to CDC regulations, puppies must meet one of the following criteria before international delivery: be at least 6 months old, have full adult teeth, or weigh at least 2.8 lbs. We monitor each puppy's development and will provide you with an estimated delivery timeline once your reservation is confirmed.

Typical delivery time from reservation to arrival ranges from 2 to 8 weeks, depending on the puppy's age and the destination country's requirements.
