About Velydog

We are committed to providing our customers with excellent and trustworthy relationships. Velydog is not a puppy mill. We either raise our puppies ourselves or work closely with reputable breeders in Korea who share our commitment to the highest health and ethical standards. We believe that every puppy deserves a loving home, and we are here to help build meaningful relationships with families.

02

Worldwide Delivery

The safety and health of our puppies are always our top priority. With over 15 years of experience in international pet delivery, we proudly maintain a 0 percent accident rate. All puppies undergo thorough veterinary checks and government quarantine inspections before being shipped to their new homes.
