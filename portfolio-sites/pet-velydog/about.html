About Velydog

Founded with a mission to connect loving families with premium teacup puppies from Korea, Velydog has grown into one of the most trusted names in international puppy adoption. Our commitment to health, ethics, and transparency sets us apart.

About Velydog

We are committed to providing our customers with excellent and trustworthy relationships. Velydog is not a puppy mill. We either raise our puppies ourselves or work closely with reputable breeders in Korea who share our commitment to the highest health and ethical standards. We believe that every puppy deserves a loving home, and we are here to help build meaningful relationships with families.

02

Worldwide Delivery

The safety and health of our puppies are always our top priority. With over 15 years of experience in international pet delivery, we proudly maintain a 0 percent accident rate. All puppies undergo thorough veterinary checks and government quarantine inspections before being shipped to their new homes.

Our Values

Ethical Breeding

We never support puppy mills. Every puppy comes from certified, reputable breeders who prioritize health and welfare above all.

Health First

Complete vaccinations, deworming, microchip identification, and a comprehensive 1-year health guarantee for every puppy.

Transparency

From video calls with your puppy to real-time tracking during delivery, we keep you informed at every stage.

Expert Care

Our team of experienced caretakers monitor every puppy 24/7, ensuring they are healthy, socialized, and ready for their new homes.

Global Reach

With over 15 years of safe international delivery experience and a 0% accident rate, distance is never a barrier.

Lifetime Support

Our relationship does not end at delivery. We provide ongoing support, advice, and guidance for the lifetime of your puppy.
