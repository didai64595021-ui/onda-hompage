Our Process

Purchasing Process

We have streamlined our purchasing process to make your experience as smooth and transparent as possible. From reservation to delivery, we are with you every step of the way.

01

Puppy Reservation

To reserve a puppy, a 50% deposit of the total payment is required. Please note that deposits are non-refundable if you cancel due to a change of mind. Once your deposit is received, we will provide you with updates, photos, and health status reports.

02

Balance Payment & Flight Scheduling

Once the flight schedule for the puppy is confirmed, the remaining 50% balance must be paid. We will inform you of the shipping details, including the airline, tracking number, and estimated arrival time.

03

Delivery Process

We ensure the safest and most comfortable travel for our puppies. Puppies travel in a temperature-controlled environment. A veterinarian-certified health certificate will be provided prior to departure. We offer real-time tracking and updates during transit.
