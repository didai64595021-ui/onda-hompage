Velydog offers FREE international delivery (airfare included). Some airports may charge a local customs fee of around $300, which is paid to the airport, not Velydog.

We begin pee pad potty training early at Velydog, but every puppy still needs time to adapt at home. We recommend continuing the training routine consistently after adoption.

We stand behind the health of our puppies. If health concerns arise after adoption, contact us immediately for support.
